About Us

Get to know us!

We are a science team formed in CEDI with the purpose of helping the world in an eco-friendly way. Our project first started off the idea of solidifying cooking oil to help its disposal, but we enventually developed our idea to what the current "Reaceitando al planeta" project is. It certainly wasn't an easy project to pull off since we weren't sure of its purpose for the first 3 months of the experiment (we were only given about 7 months), but in the end everything went relatively well. Overall, we had a good time making the experiments and learned many new things, so we hope you enjoyed our project!
